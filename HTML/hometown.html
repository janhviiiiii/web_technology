About Mira Bhayandar

Mira-Bhayander is a city and municipal corporation located in the Thane district of Maharashtra, India. Situated on the northern part of Salsette Island, it lies just before Vasai Creek and shares a border with North Mumbai. Governed by the Mira-Bhayandar Municipal Corporation (MBMC), Mira-Bhayander offers a blend of modern amenities and traditional appeal
